Senibong Cove

Senibong Cove is a waterfront paradise in Johor Bahru that offers a luxurious and peaceful escape from the hustle of the city. With world-class berthing facilities, the marina at Senibong Cove is a premier destination for yachters seeking serenity and comfort.

Located near the eastern coastline of Johor Bahru, Senibong Cove is nestled along a natural river, offering scenic views and a vibrant marina lifestyle. It's not just a marina—it's a full resort-style residential destination with cafes, a riverside promenade, and breathtaking sunsets.

Enjoy a calm anchorage, private yacht charters, and direct access to the open sea, all while being just minutes from downtown JB. Whether you're docking for the night or planning a longer stay, Senibong Cove delivers an experience tailored to luxury and relaxation.

Senibong Cove is also home to waterfront restaurants and family-friendly amenities. Boaters can enjoy access to a protected marina basin, clean water, and full docking services. This makes it a great stopover or launch point for cruising the southern waters of Malaysia.
