ABOUT US

There is a common misconception that the yachting lifestyle is out of reach for the average person. We, at Boit Luxe Marine, want to change that perception. Enter yacht charter, an affordable way to experience the thrill of the water with family and friends. We are a boating service without borders, taking you on nautical adventures in style.

Boit Luxe Marine is a major partner of Club Singapore, a luxury yacht charter company based in Singapore.

WHY CHOOSE THE BOIT LUXE MARINE?

We listen to our customers and find the right boat for their occasion. Our professional crew ensures top-quality boat rental services for a memorable experience. We offer a wide range of boats from affordable rentals to high-end luxury yachts, with transparent pricing and no hidden fees.
